Some popular Festivals

Diwali

The festival of lights, symbolizing the victory of good over evil and knowledge over ignorance.

Onam

A harvest festival celebrated in Kerala, marked by elaborate feasts, boat races, and floral decorations.

Baisakhi

A vibrant harvest festival primarily celebrated in Punjab, also marking the Sikh New Year.

Navratri

A nine-night Hindu festival celebrating the divine feminine, culminating in Dussehra.

Cuisine Highlights

Dosa

A thin, savory pancake from South India, made from fermented rice and lentil batter, often served with chutney and sambar.

Samosa

A popular fried snack with a savory filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, or lentils, found across India.

Vada Pav

A beloved Mumbai street food made with deep-fried potato dumpling (vada) served in a bread bun (pav) with chutneys.

Rasgulla

A traditional Bengali sweet: cheese dumplings cooked in light syrup.
